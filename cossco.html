about us

Rendezvous19:
Computer Science Students’ Council gives a chance to students of fresher batch to showcase their talent in different fields. Whole day is divided into different events which act as the levels. At each level students are filtered according to their performance. Eminent and esteemed personalities in respective fields judge the various skillsets of students. The one who qualifies all rounds and scores maximum in the events is crowned with the title of
“Mr./Miss Rendezvous”.


WHERE
Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering Technology, Jhansi

WHEN
11-12 January 2020
