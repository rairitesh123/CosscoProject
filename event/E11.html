News Hour Debate (NHD)

Introduction Venue

Selected students from Group discussion and Object Analysis Test will come to this round. In this event each student will be given a personality. Group of six people at a time will be seated along with one reporter. All the participants will be answering the reporter according to the personality allotted to them within given time frame. A proper debate environment will be maintained. Students qualifying this round will go for Personal Interview.
