Introduction Venue Rule Winners

“Cipher is a riddle wrapped in a mystery inside an enigma – You have to find the key”
Cipher is a hostel-based team event where each team has to find the key or the answer to the picture-based riddle. So, make the best use of your creativity, imagination and of course, the technology.

Teams will be made on a random basis. This event will continue for a few days before the final day. The team that solves the riddle in the least time possible will be awarded 10 points, the team solving second will be awarded 9 points and so on. In the end, the team getting the highest point will be declared as the WINNER. Several hints would be provided from time to time if teams aren’t able to crack the code.
